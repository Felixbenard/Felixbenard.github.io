Top 5 Exam Preparation Techniques

Date: Feb 2026

Exams can be stressful, but with the right techniques, you can maximize your study time and perform at your best. Here are the top 5 techniques:

  1. Active Recall: Test yourself regularly instead of just rereading notes.
  2. Pomodoro Technique: Study in focused intervals (25 min study + 5 min break).
  3. Mind Mapping: Visualize concepts and connections to improve memory retention.
  4. Past Papers: Practice with previous exam questions to familiarize yourself with the format.
  5. Healthy Routine: Sleep well, eat properly, and stay hydrated to keep your brain sharp.

Combine these techniques consistently to see real improvements in your exam performance.
